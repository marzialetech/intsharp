intsharp/sharpening.py

Chiu-Lin sharpening

Flux formulation:

\[ \mathbf{q} = \epsilon \nabla\psi - \psi(1-\psi) \hat{\mathbf{n}} \]

where \( \hat{\mathbf{n}} = \nabla\psi / |\nabla\psi| \) is the interface unit normal.

RHS = \( \nabla \cdot \mathbf{q} \) (divergence of the flux).

Update:

\[ \psi^{n+1} = \psi^n + \Delta t \cdot \Gamma \cdot \text{RHS} \]

Parameters:

  • eps_target: Target interface thickness \( \epsilon \)
  • strength: Sharpening strength \( \Gamma \)
