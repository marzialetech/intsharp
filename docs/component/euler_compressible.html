1D Compressible Euler

Single-phase and two-phase compressible flow with AUSM+UP flux, stiffened-gas EOS, optional MUSCL/Barth-Jespersen reconstruction.

Single-phase (3 equations)

Mass, momentum, energy conservation. Variables: ρ, ρu, E. Primitive: ρ, u, p.

Two-phase 5-equation model

Partial densities (α₁ρ₁, α₂ρ₂), mixture momentum (ρu), mixture energy (E), volume fraction (α₁). Non-conservative upwind advection for α.

Key components

  • Flux: AUSM+UP (Liou 2006) — Mach and pressure splitting
  • EOS: Stiffened gas — ideal gas (p_∞=0) or water (γ≈7.1, p_∞≈3×10⁸)
  • Reconstruction: Optional MUSCL + Barth-Jespersen limiter (2nd order)

See sod_shock_tube_1d for validation.
